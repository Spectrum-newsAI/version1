Ukraine-Russia War: International Response and Path Forward

The ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia has raised fundamental questions about international law, sovereignty, and the role of global powers in regional conflicts. The international community faces difficult choices about military aid, economic sanctions, and diplomatic solutions.

Central debates include: What level of support should democratic nations provide to Ukraine? How can the international community balance assistance with the risk of escalation? What are the most effective paths toward a sustainable resolution?
