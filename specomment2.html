Israel-Palestine Conflict: Paths to Coexistence and Peace

The Israel-Palestine conflict represents one of the world's most enduring and complex disputes, involving questions of national identity, historical claims, security concerns, and human rights. Both peoples have deep historical connections to the land and legitimate aspirations for self-determination and security.

Key questions for discussion: How can both peoples achieve security and recognition? What role should the international community play in facilitating dialogue? Are there practical steps that could build trust and reduce tensions while respecting the rights and concerns of all parties?
